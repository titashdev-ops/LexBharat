# LexBharat Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
LexBharat is committed to fostering a welcoming, respectful, and collaborative community dedicated to trustworthy legal computing.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and professional.
- Base discussions on evidence and reasoning.
- Welcome constructive feedback.
- Respect differing viewpoints.
- Focus on improving the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks.
- Deliberate misinformation.
- Disruptive or abusive conduct.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct fairly and consistently to preserve a healthy engineering community.
